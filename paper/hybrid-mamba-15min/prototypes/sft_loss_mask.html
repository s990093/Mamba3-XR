SFT Loss Mask — Token Level

Hybrid Mamba-TuckerMoE · §7.2 ChatML-only Masking

A binary mask $M_t \in \{0,1\}$ suppresses gradients on all non-answer tokens. Only the assistant answer body and the terminal <|im_end|> contribute to the SFT loss:

$$M_t = \begin{cases} 0 & x_{t+1}\in\text{user/role/header/padding}\\ 1 & x_{t+1}\in\text{assistant answer or }\texttt{<|im\_end|>} \end{cases}$$ $$\mathcal{L}_{\text{SFT}} = -\frac{1}{\sum_t M_t}\sum_{t=1}^{T-1} M_t \log p_\theta(x_{t+1}\mid x_{\le t})$$
