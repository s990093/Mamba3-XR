Causal Attention Mask

Hybrid Mamba-TuckerMoE · §5 Model Architecture

In autoregressive language modelling every query position $i$ may only attend to key positions $j \le i$. The causal mask $M$ sets the attention logit to $-\infty$ for all $j > i$, collapsing the corresponding softmax weight to zero:

$$ M_{ij} = \begin{cases} 0 & j \le i \quad \text{(attend)} \\ -\infty & j > i \quad \text{(masked)} \end{cases} \qquad \mathrm{Attn}(Q,K,V) = \mathrm{softmax}\!\!\left(\frac{QK^\top}{\sqrt{d_h}} + M\right)\!V $$
Self-attention (diagonal, $i = j$)
Past context ($j < i$, attend)
Future ($j > i$, masked to $-\infty$)

Figure: Lower-triangular causal mask for an 8-token sequence. Rows = query positions; columns = key positions. The strict lower triangle (1, attend) together with the diagonal (self) forms the causal cone each token can see.
