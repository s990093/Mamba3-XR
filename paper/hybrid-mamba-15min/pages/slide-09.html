Joint Loss Function & Z-Loss Stabilization

Following ST-MoE (Zoph et al., 2022), we combine three losses to stabilize MoE routing during training.

Total Training Objective
\[\mathcal{L} = \mathcal{L}_{\text{CE}} + \alpha \cdot \mathcal{L}_{\text{lb}} + \beta \cdot \mathcal{L}_{z}\]
1 Cross-Entropy Loss
\[\mathcal{L}_{\text{CE}} = -\frac{1}{BL}\sum_{i,t} \log P(y_{i,t} \mid x_{\leq t})\]
2 Load Balancing Loss
\[\mathcal{L}_{\text{lb}} = E \cdot \sum_{e=1}^{E} f_e \cdot \bar{p}_e\]
3 Z-Loss (Router logit stabilization)
\[\mathcal{L}_{z} = \frac{1}{B} \sum_{i=1}^{B} \left(\log \sum_{e=1}^{E} \exp(l'_{i,e})\right)^{\!2}\]

Code: torch.mean(torch.logsumexp(capped, dim=-1) ** 2)

4 Per-layer Coefficient Normalization
\[\alpha = \frac{0.1}{N_{\text{layers}} \times N_{\text{MoE}}}\] \[\beta = \frac{5 \times 10^{-3}}{N_{\text{layers}} \times N_{\text{MoE}}}\]
