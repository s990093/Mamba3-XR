Three Core Contributions

① True Hybrid Architecture

4:1 Mamba-Transformer ratio combines SSM's \(\mathcal{O}(L)\) scaling with Transformer's exact recall capability. Overcomes pure SSM limitations with minimal computational overhead.

② Tucker-Decomposed MoE

Factorizes expert weights via shared core tensor \(\mathcal{G}\) and per-expert coefficients \(U_{\text{expert}}\). Achieves 3.2× compression with < 1% quality loss. All Triton-fused for training efficiency.

③ Full-Stack Open Source

Complete pipeline from Triton training (PyTorch + custom kernels) to MLX inference (Apple Silicon). Numerically-identical cross-platform deployment with per-layer compilation.
