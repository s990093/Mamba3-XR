Overall Architecture: Hybrid Mamba

The TrueHybridMamba stacks \(N_\text{macro}\) Macro Blocks, each containing 4 Mamba-3 layers + 1 Transformer layer.

1 Hybrid Ratio
\[N_{\text{total}} = N_{\text{macro}} \times (4_{\text{Mamba}} + 1_{\text{Attn}}) = 6 \times 5 = 30\]
2 Residual + LayerScale
\[x_{l+1} = x_l + \gamma_l \cdot f_l\!\left(\text{RMSNorm}(x_l)\right)\] \[\gamma \text{ init} = 10^{-2}\]
3 Weight Tying
\[W_{\text{head}} = W_{\text{embed}}^\top \in \mathbb{R}^{d \times V}\]
4 Logit Scaling
\[\text{logits} = 30 \cdot \tanh\!\left(\frac{W_{\text{head}}\,h / \sqrt{d}}{30}\right)\]
