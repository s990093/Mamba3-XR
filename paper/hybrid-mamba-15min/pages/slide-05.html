\[B_{\text{rot}} = \text{RoPE}\!\left(\text{RMSNorm}(B) + b_B,\; \theta\right)\] \[x_{\text{ssm}} = \text{TuckerMoE}^{\uparrow}(x') \in \mathbb{R}^{H \times P \times R}\] \[\text{input} = B_{\text{rot}} \otimes x_{\text{ssm}}\]
4 Lambda Gating
\[u_t = \lambda \cdot \Delta \cdot \text{input}_t + (1\!-\!\lambda) \cdot \Delta \cdot \bar{A} \cdot u_{t-1}\] \[\lambda = \sigma(\text{Linear}(x))\]
