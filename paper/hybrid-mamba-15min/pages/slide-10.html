Learning Rate Schedule

\[\text{LR}(s) = \begin{cases} \frac{s}{W} \cdot \eta & s < W \text{ (warmup)}\\[4pt] \eta \cdot (1 - 0.2 \cdot \text{progress}) & W \leq s < W\!+\!S \text{ (stable)}\\[4pt] \eta_{\min} + \frac{0.8 - \eta_{\min}}{2}\!\left(1 + \cos\frac{\pi \cdot p}{1}\right) & \text{(decay)} \end{cases}\]

With resume re-warmup: 100 steps × 10%→100% after checkpoint load.
