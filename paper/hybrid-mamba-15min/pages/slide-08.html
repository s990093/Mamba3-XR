Router Design & Load Balancing

1 Scaled Tanh Capping (Triton fused kernel)
\[l'_{i,e} = \tau \cdot \tanh\!\left(\frac{l_{i,e}}{\tau}\right), \quad \tau = 10\]

Bounds raw logits to \([-\tau, \tau]\) — prevents numerical overflow.

2 Temperature Annealing (cosine schedule)
\[T(s) = T_{\text{end}} + \frac{1}{2}(T_{\text{start}} - T_{\text{end}})\!\left(1 + \cos\!\left(\frac{\pi \cdot s}{S}\right)\right)\]

\(T: 2.0 \to 0.5\) — high early (explore) → low late (exploit).

3 Top-K Selection & Normalization
\[p = \text{softmax}(l' / T)\] \[\hat{p}_k = \frac{p_k}{\sum_{j \in \text{top-K}} p_j + \epsilon}\]
4 Load Balancing Loss
\[\mathcal{L}_{\text{lb}} = E \cdot \sum_{e=1}^{E} f_e \cdot \bar{p}_e\]

where \(f_e\) = fraction of tokens routed to expert \(e\), \(\bar{p}_e\) = mean probability.
